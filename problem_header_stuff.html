Toggle the "Checker/QRcode" button if you would like to print off the problem (ctrl P) and use your phone to check your responses. In this case, scan the QRcode on the hard copy with your phone to display the checker. If you get stuck solving the problem, toggle to the "Problem/Basecase" button and work the base-case. Assistance in various forms is available for the base-case problem. If reflections are assigned with this problem, the "Reflections" button will be present to display or hide these questions. Press "Back" to select another problem in the assignment.
